The Idea

Our Basic Goal is to develop an Anti Theft Platform, complete with a hardware and a real time Analytics Solution, which prevents unauthorized use of your device and / or location.

Real Life use

This Anti Theft Notification system can be used with a wide range of devices and accesories, and really, the limits of it's real life use lie only in the minds and hands of the Users themselves. Attach it to your Laptop, Put it in your closet, attach it to the inside of your drawer or simpley leave it on things you don't want other people touching/ messing around with. Rest assured, you can view in real time, on any internet enabled PC or mobile phone, the current status of the movement of the device, with real time indicators as to if it's being taken by someone or not. You will also, instantly get an SMS on your phone Nummber if the Device is picked up.

Improvisation

As of now, the device needs to be connected to a network to constantly function. But don't worry, we have you covered. The board used to build this device has an inbuilt wifi and bluetooth chip that does exactly this. In the future, we are looking at attaching a snap on GSM Module which can thus run on a constant mobile connection, hence giving you freedom from the wifi network. We are also looking to include GPS functionality to help you figure out where your device is, at all times.

Features and Components

Gyroscope

The Device Features a 3-Axis , state of the art Gyroscope, with a sampling rate of 800 Mhz, which will enable you to detect even the slightest movement, and thus notify you, if your device is being touched or picked up at any point of time.

Accelerometer

The device also features a 3-Axis Acceleromtere to tell you if your device is moving/ being taken away by someone. If this does happen, you will be notified instantly and the gauges and spline charts will show motion and acceleration activity in real time.

Pubnub and Visualization

Using the PubNub data stream network, and with the help of the Javascript EON Framework, we have managed to couple the device, with beautiful and accurate real time visuals and analytics about the current state of your device. This is done with the help of Data Visualization ttols like D3.js

Instant SMS

Should someone move your device without your prior knowledge, an instant SMS is delivered to your phone with pre programmable text. This helps you detect movement in your device without even having to monitor the state of your belongings at all points of time. This is based on the Twilio SMS Infrastructure.
